# Invoice-Hour-Divider
A personal, pure-logic web utility I built to split my total working hours into multiple randomized chunks for corporate invoices.

**Why did I create it? **
Found a pain point and built a solution for it.
Our billing department requires us to submit invoices showing our hours broken down per task or project block.

However, our company time tracker doesn't actually allow separate task tracking. Manually inputting flat, uniform numbers (like writing exactly 20 hours across 4 tasks) looks fake and unrealistic. To make my invoices look normal, I had to sit down and do the math manually every single week.

You plug in your total money earned, your hourly rate, and how many tasks you need to divide the time into. The app handles the rest instantly:
It automatically varies the hours across your tasks so the invoice looks completely natural and human.
It guarantees the randomized slots always add up exactly to your total hours worked down to the last decimal point.
Features a quick "Copy" button to grab your hours instantly, and a "Clean UI" button to wipe the screen when you're done.

**Tech Stack**
HTML5 (Mostly AI) with a few small tweaks from me
Modern JavaScript (ES6) written 100% by me as part of my project-based JavaScript learning.
